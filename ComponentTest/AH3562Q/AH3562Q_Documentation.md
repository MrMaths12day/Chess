### Notes
- Omni-polar
- High Sensitivity: BOP and BRP of ±20G and ±10G Typical
- Through Hole
- 3V to 28V Operating Range
- Single Open-Drain Output with Overcurrent Limit (Pull-up resistor required)
- Good RF Noise Immunity
- Reverse Blocking Diode
